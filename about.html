About Us

This site was created in the midst of a pandemic to serve possibly unmet health and wellness needs in the broader population, as well as the incredibly selfish needs of a small group of people attending a 3-month coding bootcamp.


Our Vision

Our aim is to create a one-stop shop to access popular services related to health and wellness; sort of like an aggregator site for health and wellness.

We also hope not to get too many questions during our final project presentation.
